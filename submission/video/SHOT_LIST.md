## Editing notes

- The times are edit targets, not required raw-take lengths.
- Keep product text legible at ordinary playback speed.
- Do not animate screenshots merely to create motion; a clear still is better.
- Use the prepared fictional screenshots if a sample interaction take fails.
- Use only sanitized derivatives of real-life source media. Remove every
  third-party mark, copyrighted book cover, private screen, identifying label,
  and unapproved likeness before it reaches the timeline.
- Keep every real-life clip silent. Do not use ambient festival, gym, or cafe
  audio.
- The opening and review shots of Yoshie are silent B-roll. Do not dub English
  narration over visibly mismatched Japanese speech.
- Record collaboration evidence only from local text files and local git
  history, with every third-party logo and all branded window chrome excluded.
- Do not show a personal calendar, notification, account menu, or unpublished
  Session ID.
